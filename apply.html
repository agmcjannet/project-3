The UM Advocates are an ASUM student group dedicated to the promotion of the University of Montana. We do this by serving as all-around campus ambassadors, student peer mentors, Big Sky Experience leaders, and by embodying the Griz spirit in our everyday lives. We are a group of diverse students who represent a wide breadth of academic majors, personal interests, and beliefs. Our goal is to make everyone, from new students to alumni, feel welcomed and part of the Griz Family...while having fun doin' it!

Who are the UM Advocates?

The University of Montana Advocates organization is a 70+ member, volunteer student group that promotes the University of Montana in a variety of ways. The Advocates play a major role in many activities on campus including: new student recruitment, New Student Orientations, the First-Year Experience, homecoming, UM alumni functions, and much more. The Advocates also plan their own community service activities and social functions.

What Can the Advocates Give Me?

  • Leadership skills
  • Confidence
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Communication skills
  • Professionalism
  • The chance to work with a great team
  • A sense of ownership in the University
  • An enhanced college experience
  • Fantastic friendships, fun, and family!!!
